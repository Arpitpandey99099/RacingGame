# 🏎️ Gesture-Controlled Pro Racer

An interactive 2D car racing game built with **Python** that uses **Computer Vision** to allow players to steer, accelerate, and brake using real-time hand gestures. No keyboard required!

## ✨ Features
* **Hand Tracking:** Powered by Google MediaPipe for high-accuracy gesture recognition.
* **Dynamic Speed Control:** * 🖐️ **Open Palm:** Activates Nitro Boost (High Speed).
    * ✊ **Fist:** Applies Brakes (Slow Motion).
    * ☝️ **Index Finger:** Precise Steering.
* **Smooth Gameplay:** Implements linear interpolation for weighty, realistic car movement.
* **Progressive Difficulty:** The game speeds up as your score increases.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python 3.11+
* **Game Engine:** Pygame
* **Computer Vision:** OpenCV & MediaPipe

🎮 How to Play
Launch the game and ensure your webcam is clear.

Hold your hand up so the camera can see your fingers.

Steer: Move your hand left or right. The car follows your index finger.

Boost: Open all your fingers to turn your car gold and zoom past enemies.

Brake: Close your hand into a fist to slow down and dodge tight gaps.

Restart: If you crash, press the R key to try again.
